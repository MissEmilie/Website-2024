★ About Emilie

Emilie Brewer (she/her) is a professional Canadian LGBTQIA+, non-binary and neurodivergent voice actor specializing in video game, animation and commercial voice over. She has also done voice over for promotional videos, corporate narration, real estate narration, radio-imaging and more. Contact Emilie for a free audition or quote on your project.

Her vocal range is extremely wide, including both female and androgynous voices, boy voices, teens, 20s, 30s and 40s. Her voice has been described as warm, wispy, sultry and even bubbly. Emilie's naturally mid-toned voice enables her to voice a wide range of characters including cheerful kids, spunky or recluse teens, goth girls, valley girls, strong protagonists, big sisters, young mothers, villains and antagonists. She also can voice animals, creatures and monsters.

Emilie has provided voices for many games including visual novels, RPGs, mobile games, point-and-click adventure games, survivial horror games, fighting games and many more. Some notable games include The Hostage, Tunnel Vision, PR:Rivenverse, Radiant Force.

She is also highly experienced and trained in animation voice over including both pre-lay and ADR/dubbing. Some notable animations include Pin-up Monster, Worlds Collide.

For a full list of credits, check out Emilie's resume to learn more and feel free to check out her broadcast quality, audio engineer certified home studio, equipped with ADR capibilities and Source Connect.
